## 🔬 Featured Research

> ### 👁️ AI Image-to-Audio Description for the Visually Impaired
> **National Software Contest (NSC) — Academic Project**
> 
> An automated image-captioning system designed to help visually impaired users understand their surroundings, customized and localized for Thai culture and environments.
> 
> - **🧠 Architecture:**
>   - **Encoder:** ConvNeXt + BAM (Bottleneck Attention Module)
>   - **Decoder:** LSTM with Additive Attention *(Inspired by "Attention Is All You Need")*
> - **🇹🇭 Localization:** Specially trained on Thai food, culture, and environmental datasets.
> - **Tech Stack:** `React Native` • `Bun` • `Python` • `PyTorch`
>
